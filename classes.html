Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded froundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture), pranayama (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.

Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to agvanced students, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement-you will inhale and exhale as flow energetically through yoga poses.

Restorative Yoga
This 90 minute class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming, restorative experience is stuitable for students of any level of experience. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.
